mechismu is a leading innovating and rising club of iit(ism) dhanbad which is continuously working in the field of developing modern technology in the field of automobile. talking about the team, the team was founded in the year of 2008 and participated in baja sae india, the team got to participate in all the events but could not achieve a rank. but it was a notable performance for the first timers. next year under the guidance of dr. n. k. singh, the team finished second overall in baja sae 2010. in 2011 team decided to build a formula prototype vehicle and participated in supra india 2011. the team had a fantabulous run and it won first prize in coveted autocross event and finished fifth overall. next time, yet again, the team wet on to take part in supra sae 2012 and the team acclaimed the prize of 3rd best design among all. team shifted gear again and took a two year lapse to participate in formula design challenge 2015 and finished 6th overall.

this year team aimed at reducing the weight and increase power to weight ratio and succeded by reducing weight upto 60-70 kg, owing to the use of lighter engine and more compact bodywork.

the team is most certain that the future at mechismu is bright and innovative . mechismu is very capable of ruling the event and most certainly will leave a mark on it.
